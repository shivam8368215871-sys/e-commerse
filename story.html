Our Story

Two Dots

Comfortable. Bold. Real. That's what we stand for.

Two Dots began with one simple belief: oversized clothing should feel like freedom. We create premium, ultra-comfy streetwear that lets you express yourself without trying too hard.

Krishna

Founder

At just 20 years old, Krishna turned his passion for oversized fashion into a fast-growing brand loved by thousands.

From designing the first collection at night to packing orders with his own hands, Krishna built Two Dots from the ground up — driven by the dream of making comfort the coolest trend.

"I wanted a t-shirt that feels like a cloud but looks fire. So I made it."
